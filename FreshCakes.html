A Li'l Introducton:


Flour Chylde Bakery invites you to enjoy a sweet or savory gluten-free treat at their lovely bakery in Novato, or at one of the many Bay Area Farmers’ Markets, which they attend throughout the year.
Flour Chylde Bakery is committed to baking from scratch, in small batches, everyday with an emphasis on gluten-free. We constantly strive to ensure that each piece is crafted with care to provide our customers with the best product possible.
It was 1991 when the original Fresh Cakes & More began handcrafting cakes and freshly baked desserts using only fresh ingredients in a real kitchen. And it was no surprise when the neighborhood soon began asking for more cupcakes along with other made-to-order specialties.
Today, we continue to fulfill the needs and desires of our customers with a menu of ingredient-inspired, prepared-to-order food. When you visit our bakery, you’ll see real kitchens in place of assembly lines. You’ll hear the chopping of over 40 types of fresh fruits. You’ll see the stove’s open flame and feel the warmth from our ovens working all day long. They’re the traditions we established years ago that we still passionately follow today.
